# About me

I am currently a postdoc at KAUST in the [StochNum](https://stochnum.kaust.edu.sa/) group at KAUST led by Raúl Tempone, and my current projects focus on *data assimilation*, *model order reduction*, and *statistical model calibration*. 
Before this, I obtained my PhD at EPFL in 2025 in the [CSQI](https://www.epfl.ch/labs/csqi/) group, under the supervision of Fabio Nobile --- my thesis is entitled: "Advances in Dynamical Low-Rank Approximations for random PDEs and Data Assimilation".
Before that, I did my Masters' at the University of Edinburgh, in *Computational Applied Mathematics*. 

## Research Interests

My preferred type of research combines sound theoretical guarantees with practical implementations; I follow this philosophy when researching (and combining):

- **Model Order Reduction**, with a strong focus on *Dynamical Low-Rank Approximations* for time-dependent random or stochastic systems
- **Data Assimilation**, and especially *filtering problems* revolving around Kalman filtering and particle filters
- **Statistical Model Calibration**, in particular investigating S(P)DE-based formulations
