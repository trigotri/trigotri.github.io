# Home 

I work at the intersection of *numerical analysis* and *data assimilation*, specifically focusing on *model order reduction*, *stochastic differential equations* and *filtering problems*.

Put simply: I enjoy tackling complex problems by finding mathematical structures to leverage when coding it up --- and I want the code to be fast and reliable. 

I am currently a member of the [Stochastic Numerics](https://stochnum.kaust.edu.sa/) group at KAUST, where my projects include *Dynamical Low-Rank methods for Particle Filters* as well as *Inference for Forecast-Tracking Residual Stochastic Partial Differential Equations*.
